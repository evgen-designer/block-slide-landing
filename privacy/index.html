Privacy Policy

Your privacy is my priority

I want you to feel safe using Block Slide. This policy explains what data the app uses and, more importantly, what it doesn't do.

Who I am

App: Block Slide
Contact: support@blockslide.app

Your Data Stays On Your Device

I designed Block Slide to be a private, offline-first game. All your high scores, game progress, and settings are stored locally on your device.

  • I do not have servers that collect your game data.
  • I cannot see your strategies or playtime.
  • If you delete the app, your local progress is deleted from your device (unless backed up by standard Apple iCloud backups).

Third-Party Services

To provide certain features, I use a few trusted third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies.

  • Apple Game Center: Used to handle leaderboards and achievements. Apple manages this data.
  • AdMob / RevenueCat: If applicable, used for subscriptions or ads. They may collect anonymous identifiers to verify purchases or serve relevant ads.

Children's Privacy

Block Slide is safe for all ages, but I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If I make changes to this policy, I'll update this page.
