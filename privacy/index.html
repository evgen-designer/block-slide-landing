Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 January 2026

Your privacy is important. Block Slide is designed to respect it.

1. Data Collection

Block Slide does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No account or sign-in is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising identifiers are collected

2. Local Data

The app stores limited data locally on your device only, such as:

  • High scores
  • Game settings (e.g., haptics or bombs on/off)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

3. Third-Party Services

Block Slide does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

If you choose to rate the app or open external links (such as Terms or Privacy pages), those actions are handled by Apple or your device’s browser and are subject to their respective policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

Block Slide does not knowingly collect data from children or adults. Since no data is collected, the app is safe for all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
